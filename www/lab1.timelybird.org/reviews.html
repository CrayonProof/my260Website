Good bang for the buck. They’re not high end gallery frames/matte/glass. But, they are awesome for quickly matting, framing a photograph. The film on both sides of the plastic (not glass) takes a second to figure out how to remove, but afterwards removes easily. The frames are worth the price point, ship nicely in a box with little chance of damage in-transit to your house. Buy a few, or a few different sizes from this brand at these prices, you’ll finally frame those photographs you’ve been meaning to!

Decent looking frames that provide a simple and clean presentation. Everything about them is definitely light-weight (thin mat, thin plexiglas, thin back panel, and the actual frame is plastic). So, I wouldn't call these frames sturdy. However, for the low price and their simplicity, they work great for showing off prints and other art. I use them to present some of my 8x10" pen illustrations and watercolors. If a person wants to spend more money for a higher quality frame, that's up to them. In the meantime, they streamline my presentation process because I no longer have to deal with cutting mats and glass/plexiglas and assembling sectional frames.
